# Privacy Policy

## What is collected

**Guest login:** A display name you choose. No email or account information.

**Google login (if enabled):** Your name, email address, and Google profile ID, via OAuth. Only used to identify your account.

**Game data:** Your strategy prompt, the AI-generated rule set, and game results are stored in a Cloudflare D1 database.

**Analytics:** Anonymous gameplay events (round count, actions used, game outcomes) via Cloudflare Analytics Engine. No personal identifiers attached.

**Session:** A session token stored in a cookie, used to keep you logged in. Expires after 7 days or when you log out.

## How it's used

Data is used only to run the game. Nothing is sold or shared with third parties.

Strategy prompts are sent to Cloudflare Workers AI (`@cf/meta/llama-3.3-70b-instruct-fp8-fast`) for processing. Cloudflare's privacy policy applies to that data in transit.

## Deletion

To request deletion of your account data, email amphro@gmail.com. Include the display name or email you used to sign in.

## Self-hosted deployments

If you're running your own instance of this game, this policy does not apply. You are responsible for your own data handling.
